I'm a digital designer and developer currently at large in central London.

In 2015/16 I was part of an agile team implementing a rebrand and flexible custom publishing solutions for all British Airways triggered email communications. This comprised building complex dynamic and data driven content into HTML using ESP scripting; building responsive email and web pages; setting up and testing campaigns and data, and writing client and internal documentation.

The previous year, as part of Ebay Enterprise’s marketing agency and ESP, I worked on development projects and email campaigns for British Gas, IKEA, Gumtree, Tesco Finance, Shop Direct, Virgin Holidays, GSK, and Confused.com. As well as front end coding the role included acting as technical lead on several projects; mentoring self service clients and new team members; troubleshooting list segmentation, data hygiene, tracking and deliverability issues for all their uk clients.

In 2013 I lead a project for Net-a-Porter to strategize, design and build adaptive templates for their growing international subscriber base; the previous year I worked as a digital design consultant and coder for The Outnet working with their design team to create some of the first adaptive emails in the fashion retail industry; in 2010 I helped Mr Porter train execs in html, ESP and best practices in the build up to the business' launch and worked with them again in 2013 as a mobile UE consultant.

For five years I worked for an independent digital agency gaining experience in front end development, information architecture, copywriting, SEO and comms strategies working on a variety of brands including Setanta; Vodaphone; Porsche; Business Design Centre and Wimpy.

In 2008 I took over as creative lead/designer for a leading Porsche calendar event which continues to grow and attracted international recognition and sponsorship from Michelin and Martini in 2013.

I have over ten years experience working across industries including automotive; fashion; financial services; education; email marketing; IT services; medical; mobile networks; sports; and retail.

Whether you are an agency, marketing department or small business, I can work as part of your organization either individually or as part of a multi disciplinary project team.

If you'd like to get in touch, drop me a line.
